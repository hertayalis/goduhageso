Scarlett Johansson Is the Most Festive Holiday Party Host in Jubilant Red Dress

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Scarlett Johansson and husband Colin Jost were in joyful spirits on Thursday when they held a night of holiday festivities for all their friends. Dressing the part in jubilant red, Johansson was the most gorgeous host.

The Black Widow star was pictured in New York City’s Flatiron District looking striking in a perfect holiday-red midi dress, which she wore beneath a fuzzy white coat. To keep her rejoiceful red theme going, she accented her look with matching open-toe stiletto heels that featured a rosette detail and wide black ankle strap, a glossy purse, and a large velvet hair scrunchie around her slicked back bun. For jewelry, she opted for beautiful drop earrings, several gold rings, and a few gold bracelets. She finished off the look with glowy makeup, complete with rosy cheeks and lips.

The Saturday Night Live star looked suave walking beside Johansson in a black suit, white undershirt, and shiny dress shoes. To brave the cold, he wore a long black overcoat and matching fringe scarf.

The holiday bash was a star-studded event that saw the likes of Greta Gerwig and Noah Baumbach, Jon Hamm and Anna Osceola, Alec and Hilaria Baldwin, and Chris Evans and Alba Batista, who used the party to make their newly wedded public debut. Batista looked absolutely stunning in a red satin midi dress, black pointed-toe kitten heels, long black coat, and buttery white scarf. The Captain America star coordinated with his wife in red pants, a green sweater, brown topcoat, and brown dress shoes.

Johansson and Jost, who have been married for three years, stepped out earlier this month for another big event, the American Museum of Natural History’s 2023 gala. They posed arm-in-arm and looked so in love while wearing coordinating black-and-white outfits on their rare public date night.

John Lamparski

Johansson layered a boxy, floor-length coat in an icy white hue over a black slip dress and added pops of red with a vibrant pocket square and bright lipstick. She accented her winter look with black pointed-toe heels, a matching croc-embossed clutch, and a diamond tennis necklace. Jost looked dapper in a classic black tux and shiny dress shoes.
