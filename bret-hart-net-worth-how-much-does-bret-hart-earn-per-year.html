Bret Hart, a famous Canadian wrestler, is popularly known as Hitman among his fans. Get to know more about Bret Hart net worth and career life.

Bret Hart Biography

Bret Hart was born on July 2, 1957, in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. He is a member of the well-known Hart Family.

Bret was also trained in Hart Dungeon by his father.

He was an amateur wrestler while in school. Bret began his career with Stampede Wrestling, a promotion owned by Stu Hart. He also wrestled in New Japan Pro Wrestling for a short time.

His father, WWE Hall of Famer Stu Hart, has trained many famous wrestlers including Chris Benoit, Chris Jericho, and Edge

He has 8 brothers and 3 sisters. The brothers are Owen Hart, Allison Hart, Ross Hart, Dean Hart, Wayne Hart, Keith Hart, Bruce Hart, and Smith Hart, and the sisters are Elizabeth Hart, Georgia Hart, and Diana Hart.

Stephanie Washington is the present wife of Bret Hart.

Bret Hart is presently married to his girlfriend Stephanie Washington. Bret and Stephanie got married in 2014.

Bret Hart was first married to Julie Hart in 1982.

After the detachment from his first wife Bret married Cinzia Rota in 2004. Notwithstanding, this marriage didn’t go long as they broke up their relationship in 2007.

Bret Hart, on the other hand, has four children with his ex-wife Julie Smadu, including Jade Michelle Hart, Dallas Jeffery Hart, Alexandra 

Bret Hart joined the WWE in 1984 and founded the Hart Foundation with his brother-in-law, Jim Neidhart. They defeated the British Bulldogs in their first tag team championships. Bret concentrated on singles championships after the team split up in the 1990s. In 1991, he defeated Mr Perfect in the Intercontinental Championship. In 1992, he defeated Ric Flair to win his first WWE Championship.

Bret won the King of the Ring twice, in 1991 and 1993, becoming the only wrestler to do so. He had a long and bitter feud with Jerry Lawler over the title of King. He also had major feuds with his brother Owen and Yokozuna.

He lost the title to Bob Buckland in 1994, and Owen was to blame. Despite regaining the title, he was defeated by Shawn Michaels again. He had major feuds with Stone Cold, The Undertaker, and Sycho Sid, but his real-life feud with Vince began at the same time.

Bret left the WWE for WCW after Vince let Michaels beat him in the famous Montreal Screwjob incident.

A skilled and well-known wrestler of his caliber is bound to reap numerous benefits. Pro Wrestling Illustrated, Wrestling Observer Newsletter, BRAVO Otto, CAGE MATCH Top 10 Rankings, Hall of Fame, CAGE MATCH Year-End Awards, and WWE.com all gave him awards.

Bret Hart was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2006, but he was unable to attend the ceremony. He returned in 2009 to begin a new feud with Vince McMahon. He also won the United States Championship and was the Raw general manager at the time. Finally, in 2010, he announced his retirement.

What is Bret Hart Net Worth?

Bret Hart’s net worth is estimated to be $15 million as of March 2023.

How much does Bret Hart earn per year?

This famous wrestler amassed a large fortune over the course of his long career. His pay from WrestleMania 2 is $4,000, and his pay from WrestleMania 3 is $15,000. In 1989, Summerslam earned $10,000, and in 1992, he earned $55,000. His three-year contract with WCW Monday Nitro pays him $9,000,000.

His three-year contract with World Championship Wrestling pays him $2,500,000 per year.
