Randall Emmett's Ex Calls Out Lala Kent for Posting Her Kids on IG

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Shots fired. Randall Emmett’s ex-wife, Ambyr Childers, called out his fiancé, Lala Kent, for posting their two daughters on Instagram. On Monday, August 19, the 28-year-old uploaded several videos of Randall and Ambyr’s two kids, London, 9, and Rylee, 5, on social media. In the clip, the girls raided Lala’s makeup and decided to put it all over their face. But the Vanderpump Rules star made sure that before they left the house, the kiddos were going to take it off.

While most people thought the videos were adorable, one person wasn’t happy with Lala: Ambyr. The 31-year-old actress reposted Lala’s video and responded, writing, “Since you don’t want to answer my text messages, I’ll try here. Lala, I appreciate your love toward my children, but until you’re officially their stepmother, please refrain from posting my children on social media! I hope [you] understand where I’m coming from. Many thanks.”

The blonde beauty continued to post cute videos of the little tots throughout the day — they worked out, went to the pool and even had some down time where the girls got creative and drew a family portrait.

This isn’t the first time Ambyr has started a feud with Lala, either. In the past, the mom of two “liked” a comment on Instagram that slammed Lala for failing to be a “positive role model” for the girls. Ambyr also sided with 50 Cent after Randall and Lala got into a fight with him on Instagram. “50 Cent said it best. HOE’S WINNING … I guess if that’s what you call it. Randy got what he wanted,” she wrote after the rapper called Lala a “gold digger.”

However, it seems like the model has nothing but positive things to say about Randall’s former love. “Randall and [his ex] do such a great job of raising them, so I let them handle that part, and then I’m like, ‘Here, we have some lip gloss! Wanna do fake nails? We can do fake nails!’” she told Entertainment Tonight.

Randall, 48, and Ambyr finalized their divorce in December 2017. Less than a year later, the film producer proposed to Lala in September 2018 during a romantic trip to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. The couple is set to tie the knot in April 2020.

The Utah native couldn’t help but gush about being a bonus mom in December 2018. “Oh, I love it so much. The 5-year-old wakes me up at 6:30 in the morning, and we eat black beans and tortillas, and we pretend like we’re on a date,” she told Us Weekly at the time. “I’m like, my life is very different than what it used to be, but I’m obsessed. I’m obsessed with them.”

The Bravo personality also admitted that she wants children of her own with her future hubby. “I want two. He wants one, so we’ll have two,” she quipped.

Hopefully, Ambyr and Lala can work everything out.
