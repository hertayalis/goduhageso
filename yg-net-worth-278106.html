In 2008 he released the mixtape 4Fingaz and he went on to release the mixtapes The Real 4Fingaz in 2010, Just Re'd Up in 2011, 4 Hunnid Degreez in 2012, Just Re'd Up 2 in 2013, and Boss Yo Life Up Gang in 2013. His first single "Toot It and Boot It" (featuring Ty Dolla $ign) in 2010 reached the Top 10 of the US Rap chart. YG's 2013 single "My Nigga" (featuring Jeezy and Rich Homie Quan) was certified 2x Platinum and reached #3 on the US Rap chart.
