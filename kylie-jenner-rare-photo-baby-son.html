Kylie Jenner Shares a Rare Photo of Her Cuddling Her Baby Son

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Kylie Jenner is sharing a glimpse into her life as a mom of two.

The beauty mogul shared recent photos from her home on Instagram yesterday, including pictures of the two children she shares with longtime boyfriend Travis Scott: a baby boy, whom she welcomed in March, and four-year-old daughter Stormi Webster.

In one photo, Jenner is seen lounging by her pool in a black bikini while cuddling up close to her son—whose name she has yet to disclose to the public. In another picture, big sister Stormi and her little brother match in chocolate brown-and-tan sneakers with bright red laces. Jenner also shared a photo of Stormi's outfit, featuring her sneakers, a white T-shirt, black cargo shorts, and a mini black Gucci bag.

Last year, while launching her lifestyle brand, Kylie Baby, Jenner opened up to BAZAAR.com about her experience with motherhood.

"One of the most important things motherhood has taught me is to be gentle with yourself. Motherhood is such a balancing act, and I just try to take one day at a time," she said. "The hardest part about it is the lack of sleep [laughs]. The most rewarding thing is being able to see the world through Stormi’s eyes and watching her grow every day."

Since giving birth to her second child, Jenner has been candid about her postpartum recovery struggles.

"It's not easy mentally, physically, spiritually, it's just crazy," she said in a video posted to her Instagram Story in March. "I didn't want to just get back to life without saying that, because I think we can look on the Internet, and for other moms going through it right now, we can go on the Internet and it might look a lot easier for other people and, like, put the pressure on us, but it hasn't been easy for me either, it's been hard."
