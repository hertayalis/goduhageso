About El Pollo Loco

 News: El Pollo Loco, a well-liked eatery renowned for its delectable citrus-infused chicken, specializes in Mexican cuisine and is celebrated for its distinctive and flavorful chicken offerings.

Origin and Expansion

Originating in Mexico, El Pollo Loco later expanded its presence to the United States. The inaugural U.S. establishment debuted in 1980, and since then, the chain has burgeoned to encompass 500 outlets nationwide.

The Crazy Chicken Experience

Emphasizing quality as their top priority, El Pollo Loco takes pride in the meticulous preparation of its food. Every serving of their citrus-marinated chicken requires 55 minutes of careful attention, resulting in a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Although the citrus-marinated chicken takes center stage, El Pollo Loco presents a diverse menu showcasing a variety of Mexican favorites. Whether it’s burritos and tacos, quesadillas, or the distinctive Pollo bowl, there’s something to satisfy every palate.

Holiday Hours

Although El Pollo Loco will be closed on Christmas day, the restaurant consistently operates during other holidays. Whether it’s Thanksgiving, New Year’s, or any other festive occasion, you can rely on El Pollo Loco to be open and ready to serve their delicious food.
